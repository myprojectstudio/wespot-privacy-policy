WeSpot Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Account information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data when you use location sharing features
  • Profile information and photos
  • Messages and communications with friends
  • Event information and check-ins
  • Device information and usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Enable location sharing with your friends and family
  • Send you notifications and updates
  • Respond to your requests and support needs
  • Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Location Data

Location data is a core feature of WeSpot:

  • We only collect location data when you explicitly enable location sharing
  • Location data is only shared with friends you've added
  • You can stop sharing your location at any time
  • Location history is stored securely and can be deleted
  • We use industry-standard encryption for location data
  • Location data retention varies by subscription tier:
    • Free: 3 days
    • Shield: 30 days
    • Guardian: 90 days
    • Circle: 365 days

3.1 Subscription and Payment Data

When you purchase a subscription:

  • Payment information is processed securely through Google Play Store or Apple App Store
  • We do not store your payment card details
  • We store subscription status, tier, and expiry date for service delivery
  • Subscription data is used to provide premium features and manage your account
  • We use Play Integrity API to verify purchases and prevent fraud
  • Subscription information is stored in Firebase with encryption

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only:

  • With friends you've explicitly added to your network
  • With service providers who help us operate our services (Firebase, Google Maps, etc.)
  • When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • With your explicit consent
  • With app store providers (Google Play, Apple App Store) for subscription management
  • With emergency services when you use SOS features (location only, as required)

4.1 Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting
  • Google Maps Platform: Location services, mapping, geocoding
  • Google Play Billing / Apple In-App Purchase: Subscription processing
  • These services have their own privacy policies
  • We ensure all third-party services comply with data protection standards

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive communications
  • Secure cloud storage with Firebase
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data - Request a copy of all your data
  • Correct inaccurate information - Update your profile and account details
  • Delete your account and data - All data will be permanently deleted
  • Export your data - Receive a copy in a portable format
  • Opt-out of certain data collection - Disable location sharing, analytics
  • Withdraw consent at any time - Stop location sharing, delete account
  • Cancel subscriptions - Through app store settings
  • Request data portability - Export your location history, contacts, etc.

7. Children's Privacy

WeSpot is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
